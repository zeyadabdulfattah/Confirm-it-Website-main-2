Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

1. Free Trial Period

Confirm It offers a 7-day free trial to all new users. During this period, you have full access to our features to evaluate the service. You will not be charged if you cancel your subscription before the trial period expires.

2. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time via your account dashboard or by contacting support at basilelghandour@gmail.com. If you cancel:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
  • No further charges will be applied to your payment method.

3. Refund Policy

Because we offer a free trial to ensure the service meets your needs, payments for monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once processed. We do not offer prorated refunds for partial months of service.

Exceptions to this policy may be made at our sole discretion in cases of:

  • Proven technical errors where a charge was made in error.
  • Service unavailability exceeding 48 consecutive hours.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at info@confirmit.store.
