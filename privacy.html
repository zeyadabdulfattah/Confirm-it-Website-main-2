Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Confirm It ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you visit our website confirm-it.store or use our application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our automated confirmation services:

  • Merchant Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing information (processed securely by Paymob).
  • End-Customer Data: We process data from your e-commerce store (Shopify/WooCommerce) including customer names, phone numbers, addresses, and order details.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate automated order confirmations via WhatsApp.
  • To generate shipping labels and tracking numbers with courier providers.
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account.
  • To improve our platform's performance and fix technical issues.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your data. We share data only with third-party service providers necessary to fulfill our service functions:

  • Messaging Providers: Meta (WhatsApp API) for sending confirmation messages.
  • Logistics Providers: Bosta, Aramex, and Khazenly for order creation and shipping.
  • Payment Processors: Paymob for secure billing and subscription management.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data stored on our servers. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team at info@confirmit.store.
